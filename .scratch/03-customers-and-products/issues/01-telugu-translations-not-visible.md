## Bug: Users cannot see Telugu translations

The Customer and Product tables show a missing-translation indicator (yellow icon or green "OK" badge) but never display the actual Telugu name/place text. A user who fills in Telugu fields has no way to verify what they entered without opening the edit dialog.

The table should show Telugu text somewhere — either as a subtitle under the English name, a separate column, or an expandable row detail.
